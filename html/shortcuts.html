The following keyboard shortcuts are supported:

  • Arrows/Numpad - Move/attack
  • ESC - Cancel current action/close dialog
  • I - Open Inventory
  • M - Open Mini-map
  • T - Take all items at current location
  • S - Search
  • O - Open
  • C - Close
  • R - Rest Until Healed
  • E - Sleep Until Mana is Restored
  • D - Disarm Trap
  • < (SHIFT+,) - Climb Up Stairs
  • > (SHIFT+.) - Climb Down Stairs
  • 1-7 - Cast Spell matching appropriate toolbar button
